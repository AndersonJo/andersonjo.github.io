# 1. Basic Geometry 

## 1.1 Degree and Radian 

$$ \begin{align}
Radian &= Degree \time \frac{\pi}{180} \\
Degree &= Radian \time \frac{180}{\pi} \\
2\pi Radians &= 360^o \\
\pi Radians &= 180^o
\end{align} $$
